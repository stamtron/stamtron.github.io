Subsea pipeline inspections produce vast amounts of video data. This paper evaluates the performance of 2D and 3D Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) in capturing both spatial patterns and temporal features from subsea inspection video streams, identifying key trade-offs in accuracy and computation.
